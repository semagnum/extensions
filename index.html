My Official Extensions Repository

Welcome! Here you can add my repository in Blender 4.2 to access all my free extensions.

Just add the following URL in Blender as a remote repository:

https://spencermagnusson.com/extensions/add_ons/index.json

If you need them offline, you can download them individually, either from their own repositories or the zip files included in this repository.

Includes

nView Lite, a demo version of nView to simplify camera-based scene optimization.

Shot Matcher to simplify basic color matching for compositing visual effects

BLint for simplified project standardization and quality checks

Color From Material to quickly generate viewport materials colors based on the shader nodes

Scene Complexity to analyze the complexity of your scene and make better optimization decisions
